Interested in joining?

We are looking for motivated researchers at all levels to join our group! Please send inquiries to Marie Suver. Postdoctoral and research technician candidates should include a cover letter describing your research interests, a CV, and contact information for three references. Prospective graduate students can enter through one of the following graduate training programs at Vanderbilt: the Biological Sciences Graduate Program, the Interdisciplinary Graduate Program, or the Neuroscience Graduate Program.
